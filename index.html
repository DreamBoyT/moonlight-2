FAQ

1 Are there any placement opportunities provided within the campus?

Yes, the college organizes placement drives regularly, with many core companies offering internships and employment opportunities.

2 What kind of campus events are organized?

The college hosts various events like symposiums, technical lectures, and sports activities to foster overall development.

3 How is the hostel fee structured?

The hostel fee is INR 100,000 per year, which covers accommodation and basic facilities.

4 Is financial assistance available for students?

Yes, the college offers scholarships based on merit and financial need, catering to students from diverse backgrounds.

5Can students go out of the campus during weekends?

Yes, students are allowed to go out with proper authorization, ensuring their safety and well-being.

6How are disciplinary matters handled in the hostel?

The hostel has well-defined rules and regulations enforced by the warden and administration, ensuring a conducive living environment for all residents.
