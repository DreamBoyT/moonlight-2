About Us

Discover the Heart of SVCE Hostel Life

Vibrant Student Campus Life

Visit SVCE

Welcome to our college hostel, a haven for students seeking a balanced and enriching residential experience. We offer essential amenities, quality education, and a serene campus environment.


Hostel Block and Mess Halls

Our hostel rooms are furnished with essential furniture and feature good ventilation and lighting. Each floor is equipped with bathrooms, toilets, and hot water through a geyser or solar heater. Emergency power is available via two standby diesel generators. Separate mess halls for gents and ladies boast highly mechanized kitchens serving a pure vegetarian menu, predominantly South Indian, revised by students each semester.


Governance

Hostel governance is managed by a Warden and office staff, with a Welfare Committee, including student and management representatives, meeting monthly to enhance hostel life. The Dean of Student Welfare and Maintenance Department reps also participate as needed.


Highlights

Our college offers excellent placement opportunities with numerous recruiters visiting the campus. The peaceful, green surroundings create an ideal learning environment. We pride ourselves on delivering quality education through our outstanding faculty and staff. Additionally, our state-of-the-art infrastructure includes well-equipped labs, modern classrooms, and extensive sports facilities.
